# Wiki index

The always-loaded map. Keep it thin: link to pages, never paste long content or
inline the catalog here. Two hops max — index to a page, page to a source.

## How loading works

- Eager (every session): this `index.md` and the recent window of `journal.md`.
- On demand (follow a link): `catalog.md`, anything under `raw/`, `pages/`, and
  the sealed archives under `log/`. Nothing else enters context.

## Content map

- `journal.md` — dated activity log, recent window. Older months sealed under `log/`.
- `catalog.md` — index of raw captures under `raw/` (read on demand).
- `pages/` — maintained synthesis pages. Listed below; each is pulled only when relevant.
- `raw/` — immutable dated captures and long traces. Catalogued in `catalog.md`.
